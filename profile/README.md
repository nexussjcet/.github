# The Nexus Project, SJCET
The Nexus Project, SJCET was formed on 12 September, 2018 by a few free thinkers to support and organize Free/Libre and Open-Source Software and Hardware related activities in [NEXUS SJCET](http://nexus.sjcetpalai.ac.in/). 

We support:
- The freedoms of:
  -  Speech
  -  Press
  -  Association on the Internet
- The Right to:
  - use, study, copy, modify, and redistribute computer programs  
  - study, modify, distribute, make, and sell the design or hardware based on that design.
  - write software and engineer hardware unimpeded by private monopolies
